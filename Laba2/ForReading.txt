The Last of Us
TV Series

The Last of Us is an American post-apocalyptic 
drama television series created by Craig Mazin 
and Neil Druckmann for HBO. Based on the 2013 
video game developed by Naughty Dog, the series 
is set in the present day, twenty years after a 
mass fungal infection caused by a mutation in 
the genus Cordyceps sparked a global pandemic.
